Burger

Delicious, juicy homemade burger with simple ingredients, a flavorful patty, and customizable toppings.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2–3 burger buns, toasted lightly
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions (raw or caramelized)
  • Pickles or cucumber slices
  • Cheese slices (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • Patty
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise (as preferred)

RECIPE

  1. Lightly toast the buns on a pan or grill for 1–2 minutes for a crisp exterior and enhanced flavor.
  2. Spread your choice of sauce on the bottom bun
  3. Add lettuce, the cooked patty with melted cheese, tomato slices, onions, and pickles.
  4. op with sauce and cover with the top bun. Press gently to hold everything together.
